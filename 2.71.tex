Given an alphabet of $n$ symbols, where the relative frequencies of the
symbols are $1, 2, 4, ..., 2^{n-1}$, trees of the following form are produced:
\\
\\
For $n = 5$:\\
\\
\begin{forest}
[('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$ $c_4$ $c_5$) 31)
  [($c_5$ 16)]
  [('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$ $c_4$) 15)
    [($c_4$ 8)]
    [('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$) 7)
	 	  [($c_3$ 4)]
		  [('($c_1$ $c_2$) 3)
		    [($c_2$ 2)]
		    [($c_1$ 1)]
      ]
    ]
  ]
]
\end{forest}
\\
\\
For $n = 10$:\\
\\
\begin{forest}
[('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$ $c_4$ $c_5$ $c_6$ $c_7$ $c_8$ $c_9$ $c_{10}$) 1023)
  [(J 512)]
  [('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$ $c_4$ $c_5$ $c_6$ $c_7$ $c_8$ $c_9$) 511)
    [(I 256)]
    [('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$ $c_4$ $c_5$ $c_6$ $c_7$ $c_8$) 255)
      [($c_8$ 128)]
      [('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$ $c_4$ $c_5$ $c_6$ $c_7$) 127)
        [($c_7$ 64)]
        [('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$ $c_4$ $c_5$ $c_6$) 63)
          [($c_6$ 32)]
          [('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$ $c_4$ $c_5$) 31)
            [($c_5$ 16)]
            [('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$ $c_4$) 15)
              [($c_4$ 8)]
              [('($c_1$ $c_2$ $c_3$) 7)
                [($c_3$ 4)]
                [('($c_1$ $c_2$)  3)
                  [($c_2$ 2)]
                  [($c_1$ 1)]
                ]
              ]
            ]
          ]
        ]
      ]
    ]
  ]
]
\end{forest}
\\
\\
In a tree of this form, 1 bit is required to encode the most frequent symbol and $n-1$ bits are required to encode the least frequent symbol.
